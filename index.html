Treaty of Waitangi
overview
------The Treaty of Waitangi, the founding document of New Zealand, shaping New Zealand's history and relations.
The Treaty of Waitangi, signed in 1840, aimed to establish peace and cooperation between Māori and the British Crown. It addressed land rights, governance, and protection of Māori interests. However, its interpretation and implementation have been subject to debate, impacting New Zealand's history and society.
